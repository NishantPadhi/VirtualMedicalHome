“VIRTUAL MEDICAL HOME” is a site designed keeping in mind the different medical procedures/stages that one has to go through during various medical treatments.Virtual Medical Home System is a web application which provides online medical services to everyone at their doorstep.

The users living in metro or remote village can connect through internet to get these services. This web application is more effective, quick in providing medical help especially to people in villages where very few doctors are present. This helps the patients to maintain a neat health record and to lead a healthy life.

Virtual Medical Home is a web application which provides online medical services to everyone at their doorstep.

There are three basic users - Patient, Admin and Doctors. All users have their own profiles in VMHS.

Patients can search for doctor and make online appointments. They also can view their health record, lab reports, doctor’s prescription and medical expenses. Patient can also register in system.Patient can send and receive mail.

The second user is Doctor. Doctor’s can give appointments, prescription and can view patient’s health record. Doctor can send and receive mail. Doctor can see daily schedule.

Admin has the authority to add/update/delete patrient and doctor. Admin can send mail to the doctor and patient. The users living in metro or remote village can connect through internet or approach nearby kiosk to get these services.
